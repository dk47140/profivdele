About “T&T Group”

We don’t create just a design, we consult what needs to be done to make your project succeed.

We know that client loyalty is our top priority.

Welcome to “T&T Group”. We are interior design Company based in Moscow, where we specialize in creating bespoke, sophisticated and timeless living spaces that are tailored to your unique style and vision. Since 2005, we have been creating turnkey design projects for residential and commercial spaces – from measurements to final decor.

Our team of highly skilled and experienced professionals is committed to delivering personalized attention and outstanding service tailored to your needs. We take pride in our ability to listen intently, understand your preferences, and transform them into a stunning and functional design that exceeds your expectations. From the initial concept to the final touches, we collaborate with you at every stage to ensure your vision realized in every detail. With over 19 years in the industry, we have successfully completed more than 500 design projects and implemented over 300 with exceptional results. Our expertise and creativity spans a wide range of styles and settings, from warm and inviting family homes to chic penthouses and modern offices.

Our primary goal is to help you achieve your dream space while staying within your budget. Leveraging our extensive experience, we know how to execute interior design projects more efficiently and cost-effectively, ensuring exceptional results with every endeavor. Whether you envision a cozy or a sophisticated space, we have the expertise to bring your dreams to life and exceed your expectations.

Work process

Stages of work

We believe that quality interior design has the power to transform spaces and lives. It is about creating environments that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also functional and life enhancing.

Interior design concept

Our journey begins with a deep dive into your world. We listen to your stories, understand your needs, and visualize your aspirations. After the client manager answers all your questions, we sign an agreement and you make an advance payment. Next, we take measurements, photos and we fill out the technical task.

Read more

Draft design

Based on the characteristics of the room, ergonomic requirements and your needs, we develop an optimal planning solution. An interior collage will also be compiled to present the basic concept and overall style of the interior for each room. At this stage, we will decide on materials, lighting and furniture.

Read more

Visualization

With the help of visualization, you will understand what the interior will look like after its implementation. This stage will save you from disappointments and wrong decisions or, on the contrary, will confirm the right choice.

Read more

Working documentation

Development of working documentation on the agreed concept plan (according to the list in the contract). All necessary drawings (statements, plans and layouts) will be prepared for the implementation of your project.

Read more

Implementation of the design project

We will monitor compliance with the accuracy of the implementation of all your ideas and accompany the project in the selection of furniture, materials and equipment. We coordinate with contractors, manage timelines, and oversee the quality to ensure that the reality matches your vision.

Read more

Control

Control by the designer over compliance with the accuracy of the implementation of all the author's ideas during the construction process. It is possible to travel to the site in any city in any country. Conditions are discussed individually.

Read more

Equipment

Full support of the customer in completing the interior with materials, furniture and equipment necessary for the implementation of the planned project. The stage runs in parallel with the preliminary design.

Read more

Decoration

Decoration is the final touch that will add comfort, personality, and atmosphere to your interior. It encompasses the selection and placement of accessories such as throw pillows, rugs, artwork, and decorative objects that enhance the overall design while reflecting your personal style.

Read more

Photo and video shooting

Photo and video shooting are essential services for showcasing the beauty and detail of completed interior projects. These images are ideal for printing in design books, magazines, and other social media, as well as for use on social networks to attract and inspire potential clients.
